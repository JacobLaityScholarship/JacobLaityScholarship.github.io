About

I have been coding for 3 years.

I started coding by making simple games in Scratch. Now I am trying to win an ICT scholarship.

I enjoy programming, researching the latest tech, flying my drone and playing video games.

I am a big Star Wars fan. I am looking forward to Episode VIII.

Why did I start to become interested in tech?

My first experience with code was when my father told me about Scratch, as he thought it would be a good idea for me to learn to code. I didn't really use it that much but I liked the game-element to it. I think what really got me hooked was when I found out that if I learned to code that I could make Minecraft mods.

Why do I like tech so much?

This is a tricky question, but I have narrowed it down to two main points: Firstly, The sense of achievement when your idea works or you fix that error that has been holding you back for weeks is immense. Secondly, I love how the entire industy can change overnight and how everything never stays the same. I think the reason I like this ever-changing industry is because it is a contrast to my daily school-life of routine.
