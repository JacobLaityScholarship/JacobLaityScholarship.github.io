Scratch

Scratch is a free educational programming language that was developed by the Lifelong Kindergarten Group at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology.

A quick review

Scratch is a drag and drop programing language that mostly young people use for learning but it can get very boring if you are good at it. It can also be used as basic UI designer. It is a good beginners language though and you can make some cool things on it
