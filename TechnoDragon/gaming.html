Blockstorm

Although this game is not the most popular one out there it is extremely addictive and fun. There is hours upon hours of amazing content for a fiver

Terraria

This game isn't too popular, but it is a legendary game, many people have the misconception between 8 bit modeling and bad graphics, this is the only criticization i've heard, but besides that I think it is a very good game. It has hundreds of hours of content for 6 pounds

Assassins Creed

These video games are based on historical events and accuracy. This is why I love them, yes I am a massive history nerd!

Geometry Dash

Ok seriously some people automatically assume that this game is bad becuase it is 2D. Well let me tell you - this game is amazing! For about 2 pounds you are receiving 20 amazing levels and thousands of community made maps. For 2 pounds!

Call of Duty

I hate this game. I just dont enjoy it - unrealistically fast-paced combat. Some people love it some people hate it

ArmA 3

Essentially this game is the exact opposite to call of duty. This is a 'military simulator'. A super realistic FPS and Third Person Shooter. Also it has an amazing multiplayer RPG gamemode called Altis Life where you can play as a cop, civilian or a rebel.

Total War

This is an amazing top - down strategy game. I think the age ratings are a bit unfair - it is a 16 becuase of the incredible difficulty of the game. There is absolutely no swearing or gore! Other than that this top down - historical war strategy game is fantastic

Rust

This a fantastic multiplayer survival game. It can be very frustrating when someone raids your base and steals all your stuff but other than that this game is a masterpiece!
