BrickPi Car

A Raspberry Pi powered lego car that uses a Mindstorms and BrickPi board.
Click here to see the robot driving!

The BrickPi is a modification for the RPI that lets you use Lego Mindstorms EV3 sensors and motors with the RPI and Python!

You can control the bot with the ‘w’,’a’ ,’s’, ‘d’ keys
It is a 2 wheeled robot with a very easy to build chassis.
