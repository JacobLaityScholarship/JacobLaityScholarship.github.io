My current project is...

A survival game built in the Unity Engine

Up to 20 players are trapped on an island. They can choose to form teams and fight together, or go rougue and attack others. The goal is to survive. If you die, you lose all your gear and you start again. In the start of the game, the player will struggle to survive, slowly building up resources and a base, later in the game, the player will have access to stronger weaponry to raid and fight other players and their bases. It is still in early development.
